BUSINESS OVERVIEW

Tesla designs, manufactures, and sells electric vehicles, energy generation and storage products, software-enabled features, and related services. Results are sensitive to vehicle delivery volumes, average selling prices, product mix, regulatory credits, energy storage deployments, and manufacturing efficiency.

AUTOMOTIVE PERFORMANCE

Automotive revenue was affected by vehicle delivery volumes, average selling prices, product mix, regulatory credit revenue, and foreign exchange. Price reductions and mix changes pressured automotive gross margin, partly offset by manufacturing cost reductions.

ENERGY GENERATION AND STORAGE

Energy generation and storage revenue increased due to higher deployments of battery storage products. Profitability improved with scale, factory utilization, and cost reductions, though results remained sensitive to project timing.

RISK FACTORS

Risks include intense competition, pricing pressure, manufacturing ramp execution, battery cell supply, regulatory changes, dependence on key personnel, autonomous driving development uncertainty, product quality issues, and demand sensitivity to interest rates.
