BUSINESS OVERVIEW

Exxon Mobil participates in upstream, downstream, chemical, and lower-emission investment activities. Results are sensitive to commodity realizations, production volumes, refining and chemical margins, project execution, and capital discipline.

UPSTREAM PERFORMANCE

Upstream earnings were primarily affected by crude oil and natural gas realizations, production volumes, and operating expenses. Higher production from advantaged assets partly offset weaker commodity prices in certain periods.

RISK FACTORS

Risks include volatility in crude oil, natural gas, and refined product prices; changes in demand; reserve replacement uncertainty; project execution risk; climate-related regulations; litigation; and geopolitical events affecting operations and supply chains.

CAPITAL ALLOCATION

Capital allocation priorities included investing in advantaged projects, maintaining a strong balance sheet, paying a reliable dividend, and repurchasing shares when supported by cash flow and market conditions.
