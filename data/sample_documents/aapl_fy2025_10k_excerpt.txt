BUSINESS OVERVIEW

Apple designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables, accessories, and services. The company monetizes its installed base through device sales, advertising, cloud services, payment services, subscriptions, and digital content.

NET SALES

Services net sales increased due to growth in advertising, cloud services, payment services, and subscriptions. Management said services growth was supported by the active installed base of devices and customer engagement across its platforms.

GROSS MARGIN

Services gross margin was higher than products gross margin. Total gross margin benefited from a higher services mix, while products gross margin remained sensitive to component costs, foreign exchange movements, and product mix.

RISK FACTORS

Risks include dependence on new product introductions, competitive pressure in smartphones and services, supply chain concentration in Asia, regulatory scrutiny of app distribution and payment practices, foreign exchange volatility, and potential disruptions from component shortages.
